MLSS Senegal 2025

The Machine Learning Summer School (MLSS) has been hosted in countries around the globe, gathering top experts and emerging talent in artificial intelligence and machine learning. This year, we’re excited to bring MLSS to West Africa for the first time, with Senegal as our host country. The program offers PhD students from around the world a unique opportunity to dive into the fundamentals of machine learning and explore the latest cutting-edge techniques in the field.
